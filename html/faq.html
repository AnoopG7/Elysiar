Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Elysiar services

How do I create an Elysiar account?

→

To create an Elysiar account, download our app or visit our website and click on "Sign Up". You'll need to provide your name, email address, phone number, and create a password. Once you've verified your email and phone number, your account will be active and ready to use.

How do I reset my password?

→

To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password securely.

How do I book a ride?

→

Booking a ride with Elysiar is simple:

  1. Open the app or visit our website
  2. Enter your pickup location and destination
  3. Select the type of vehicle you prefer
  4. Review the estimated fare and arrival time
  5. Confirm your booking

You'll receive confirmation of your booking and can track your driver in real-time.

Can I schedule a ride in advance?

→

Yes, you can schedule rides up to 7 days in advance. On the booking screen, tap on "Schedule" instead of "Request Now", select your preferred date and time, and complete your booking. You'll receive reminders before your scheduled ride, and a driver will be assigned when it's time for your pickup.

What payment methods are accepted?

→

Elysiar accepts various payment methods including:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Elysiar credits
  • Cash (in selected regions)

You can manage your payment methods in the "Payment" section of your account.

How do I get a receipt for my ride?

→

After completing a ride, you'll automatically receive a receipt via email. You can also view and download all your ride receipts from the "Trip History" section in your account. Each receipt includes details about the trip, fare breakdown, and payment method used.

What safety measures does Elysiar have in place?

→

Elysiar prioritizes your safety through several measures:

  • Thorough background checks for all drivers
  • Real-time trip tracking and sharing
  • In-app emergency assistance button
  • Driver and vehicle photo verification before pickup
  • Contactless ride options
  • Anonymous phone number masking for privacy
  • Post-ride feedback and rating system

All rides are also insured and monitored for your safety and peace of mind.

How can I report an issue with my ride?

→

If you experience any issues during or after your ride, you can report them through the following channels:

  • In the app: Go to "Trip History", select the relevant trip, and tap "Report an Issue"
  • Website: Log in to your account, visit "Help" and select "Report a Problem"
  • Contact support directly through our 24/7 helpline

Our support team will investigate the matter and respond within 24 hours.
